
* Running multiple instances of `nmap` at once:
    - Whenever I run multiple instances of `nmap` at once, all scans run
        significantly slower, and I'm never sure why. This note lists
        possible answers.
    - https://stackoverflow.com/a/24994879
    - https://nmap.org/book/reduce-scantime.html
        * "Five or ten Nmap processes are fine, but launching 100 Nmap
            processes at once is not recommended."
